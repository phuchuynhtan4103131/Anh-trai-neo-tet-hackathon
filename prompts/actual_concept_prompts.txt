# Initial Game Concept
"Create a web-based platformer game that teaches about environmental and social issues in Australia and Vietnam while providing engaging gameplay."

# Educational Theme Integration
"Integrate educational content about:
- Climate change impacts on communities
- Social inequality and access to services
- Environmental conservation
- Urban development challenges
- Cultural preservation and adaptation"

# Level Design Concepts
"Design levels that:
- Progress in difficulty
- Integrate quiz elements naturally
- Teach about environmental issues
- Provide checkpoints for progress
- Balance challenge and learning"

# Game Flow
"Create a game progression that:
- Starts with basic mechanics
- Gradually introduces environmental concepts
- Provides clear feedback
- Rewards learning and skill
- Maintains player engagement"
