How to Play

Controls:

← or A: Move Left

→ or D: Move Right

↑ or SPACE: Jump

Gameplay:

• Jump and hit question blocks to answer environmental questions

• Correct answers give you rewards (health or gold)

• Wrong answers reduce your health

• Reach checkpoints to save your progress

• Complete levels by reaching the end

Health: 100 Gold: 0 Level: 1
